1. Cancellation by You

All cancellations must be submitted in writing via email to bookings@mylandtours.co.ke. Cancellation fees are calculated from the date we receive your written notice:

1. Insurance

Comprehensive travel insurance isn't mandatory for all My Land Tours and Travel safaris. Though we strongly recommend that all travelers to have a comprehensive policy. You must provide proof of insurance before departure on Mount Kenya climbs, Adventure Tours, Some international trips, Specific partner requirements or when a traveller has any pre-existing conditions that necessitate coverage. Without valid insurance, we reserve the right to cancel your booking with standard cancellation fees applying.

2. Coverage

Your travel insurance should include at minimum if insured:

  • Emergency Medical: Coverage sufficient for hospitalization, emergency treatment, and medical emergencies during travel.
  • Medical Evacuation: Coverage for emergency evacuation and transportation from remote destinations to appropriate medical facilities in case of emergencies.
  • Trip Cancellation: 100% of prepaid non-refundable costs
  • Trip Interruption: 100% of remaining trip value
  • Baggage & Personal Effects: Adequate coverage for cameras, binoculars, etc.
  • 24-Hour Emergency Assistance: With reverse charge call capability

3. Safari-Specific Coverage

Standard travel insurance often excludes safari activities. Ensure your policy explicitly covers: wildlife viewing from open vehicles; walking safaris and bush walks; hot air balloon rides; and activities above 4,000m (Mount Kenya treks).

4. Medical Considerations for Kenya

While Kenya offers excellent private medical facilities in major cities, travelers visiting remote safari destinations should ensure their insurance includes emergency medical evacuation coverage. Quality private hospitals exist in major cities but evacuation from remote parks is expensive. Malaria is present in most safari areas — take prophylaxis and use mosquito repellent. Drink only bottled or purified water provided by your tour operator.

5. Recommended Insurance Providers

While we don't endorse specific providers, our clients have had positive experiences with: World Nomads; SafetyWing; Allianz Global Assistance; and IMG Global. For Kenyan residents, APA Insurance and Jubilee Insurance offer suitable local coverage and Amref in general.

6. Pre-Existing Conditions

Declare all pre-existing medical conditions when purchasing insurance. Failure to disclose may void your coverage. Many insurers offer waivers if you purchase within 14-21 days of your initial trip deposit.

7. Claims Assistance

In the event of a claim, we will provide all necessary documentation including: trip invoices; medical reports; police reports (if applicable); and witness statements. Contact us immediately if you need assistance with a claim.

8. Emergency Assistance During Your Trip

If you experience a medical emergency, accident, loss of travel documents, or any situation requiring immediate assistance, contact your insurance provider's emergency hotline first and notify the company guide or driver assigned to your trip as soon as possible. Our team will assist in coordinating local support where possible.
